About

Background and why this work is personal.

I’m a born-and-raised New Yorker, originally from Queens and now rooted in the Hudson Valley, where I first got involved in politics volunteering for State Senator James Skoufis’s reelection campaign. I’ve led at every level — local, state, caucus, and national — building chapters, raising funds, and expanding youth political infrastructure across the country.

My commitment to this work is personal: I grew up in a working-class immigrant family where politics wasn’t theory — it shaped whether we could afford insulin, groceries, or stability. Those experiences taught me that leadership is responsibility, and that survival should not be a prerequisite for opportunity.

Goal: Ensure that tomorrow’s party truly starts today — on every campus in New York State.
